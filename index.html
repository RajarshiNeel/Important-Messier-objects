🎓 Prediction Methodology

Our prediction values are computed using a weighted score combining:

  • 1. Naked-Eye Visibility (30%): Crucial for raw observation questions where telescope-less navigation is required.
  • 2. Astrophysical Context (40%): High probability for questions requiring math calculations (pulsars, Cepheids, dust extinction, jeans mass).
  • 3. Historical Frequency (30%): Frequency of appearance in global IOAA and national-level TST practicals.
